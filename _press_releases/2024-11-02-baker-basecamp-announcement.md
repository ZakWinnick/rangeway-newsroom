**SAN FRANCISCO, CA - November 2, 2024** - Rangeway Energy today announced Baker Basecamp, its flagship electric vehicle charging facility set to open in 2026 on California's I-15 at CA-127. The location will serve as the gateway charging hub for travelers exploring Death Valley National Park and the LA-Vegas corridor.

## Indoor Comfort Guaranteed

Unlike traditional EV charging stations that leave travelers waiting in parking lots, Baker Basecamp features a 3,000 square foot climate-controlled indoor lounge, premium café, and six ultra-fast 350kW+ DC chargers. The facility will be staffed 24/7 by Rangeway's Trailkeepers, ensuring reliability and hospitality at every stop.

"We're solving the fundamental problem with EV road trip charging: it's designed for cars, not people," said [CEO Name], CEO of Rangeway Energy. "Every Rangeway location will guarantee indoor comfort, whether it's 95°F in the desert or freezing in the mountains. We're making EV travel to America's most beautiful places preferable, not just possible."

## Strategic Location

Baker Basecamp is positioned 90 miles from Los Angeles and 90 miles from Las Vegas, serving as the perfect midpoint charging stop for the 1.1 million annual visitors to Death Valley National Park. The location addresses a critical infrastructure gap on one of California's most scenic corridors.

## The Rangeway Network Model

Baker Basecamp represents the first of Rangeway's regional cluster approach to scenic corridor charging infrastructure:

- **Basecamps**: Full-service destination hubs with 6 ultra-fast chargers, 3,000 sq ft lounges, premium cafés, and 24/7 staffed operations
- **Waystations**: Automated locations with 4 ultra-fast chargers and climate-controlled lounges

## About Rangeway Energy

Rangeway Energy is building America's premier electric vehicle charging network for scenic corridors. Every Rangeway location features climate-controlled indoor lounges, premium cafés, and ultra-fast charging infrastructure—eliminating the parking lot waits that plague traditional EV charging. Starting with Baker Basecamp on California's LA-Vegas corridor, Rangeway is making EV road trips to America's most beautiful destinations preferable, not just possible.
